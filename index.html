Director vs. Manager Signals
On impact language:
MANAGER

"We deployed 50 bots and saved a lot of time."

DIRECTOR

"325K hours recaptured — equivalent to 156 FTEs — without a single layoff conversation."

On governance:
MANAGER

"We had approval processes for new bots."

DIRECTOR

"I chaired a monthly governance board with CIO, CFO, and BU Presidents — every exception escalated to me."

On failure:
MANAGER

"It was a challenging situation but we recovered."

DIRECTOR

"The bot failed. I owned it publicly, ran the post-mortem myself, and rebuilt the exception-handling layer. It hasn't failed since."
